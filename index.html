About

The Oakland Robotics Association, ORA is an Oakland University Student ran club which specializes in Autonomous Robotics. With a long running history and various projects, ORA has been and active hub for future engineers.

ORA Competes in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition, where we enter an automous robot to drive in a road like course purely autonomously. The robot is built from ground up, with a custom designed frame, electrical system and software stack.

Oakland Robotics is proud to represent Oakland University in various outreach events. From tours of the lab, to workshops, ORA gives back to our community and is proud to inspire the next generation of engineers.
